# Privacy Policy — Atlas

_Last updated: June 11, 2026_

Atlas ("the extension") links UWorld question results to the user's own Anki
flashcard collection.

## Data collection

Atlas does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The extension
sends no information to the developer or to any third party or external server.

## Local storage

Atlas saves a small set of preferences on your own device using the browser's
storage API — your selected exam, and your dark-mode and expected-score toggles.
This stays on your device and is never transmitted.

## Local communication

To open your flashcards, Atlas communicates only with Anki running on your own
computer (through the companion "Atlas Bridge" add-on) over the loopback address
`127.0.0.1:8765`. This traffic never leaves your machine.

## Page access

On UWorld pages, Atlas reads the on-page content needed to build a matching Anki
tag search. This processing happens locally in your browser and is not stored or
sent anywhere.
